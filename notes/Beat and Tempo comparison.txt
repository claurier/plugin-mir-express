Beat / Tempo Comparison

-TempoTap (from MIRLib): does not provide reliable results. The algorithm appears to be unfinished.

-BTrack: provides a good tempo estimation. However, the beat indicator does not seem to work properly. There may be a latency issue that was inadvertently introduced.

-beat_this_cpp: provides the best results. The tempo estimation is close to perfect (on simple examples), performing better than the others, with fewer octave errors and more precise tempo estimation.
